How it works

Four steps. Zero ad spend.

01
Recruit Ambassadors
Clients, friends, bartenders, stylists — anyone who talks about your business joins your network and gets a personal referral link and QR code instantly.
02
They Share in Conversation
No posts. No campaigns. Just a person pulling out their phone mid-conversation and letting someone scan. That's the whole move.
03
New Client Discovers You
The referral page shows your business, your work, your booking link. The customer can act immediately — consultation, booking, inquiry.
04
Ambassador Gets Rewarded
When the referral converts, earnings go into their WOTS wallet. Transfer to their bank or spend it back with you as House Credits. The loop closes itself.
The WOTS difference

The referral fee only exists because it already worked.

Google charges for clicks. Facebook charges for impressions. Neither guarantees a human walks through your door. With WOTS, you only spend money when a real customer already put money in your pocket. Your ambassador earns store credit and spends it back with you. You paid out $20 and got a $150 return visit. You got paid twice on the same referral. That's not advertising. That's a growth engine.
