The best referral
Work The Room
In the real world

You don't need to spam your link or post it everywhere. The most effective referrals come from real moments — someone mentions they've been thinking about getting a tattoo, or you notice their old piece and ask if they've thought about adding to it. That's your opening. Tell them about your experience at the studio, show them some of the work, and offer to send them your link so they go through you. A personal recommendation from someone they trust is worth more than any ad. You're not selling — you're just connecting people to something you already believe in.

Common questions
FAQ
Do they have to use my link for me to get credit?
Yes. The link is what ties the referral to you. If they go directly to the studio without clicking your link, we have no way to credit you. Always send the link first.
How do I know if someone used my link?
Check your ambassador dashboard at wotsworks.com/a/?ref=[your code]. It shows every referral linked to you and the current status — scanned, claimed, or converted.
When exactly do I get paid?
After the tattoo session is completed and paid for, the reward lands in your WOTS wallet. Not when they click, not when they book — when the ink is done and the money is in. Then you choose: cash out or convert to Blood Money.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?
No limit. Refer one person or fifty — every completed tattoo that came through your link earns you a reward.
What if they don't go through with the tattoo?
No tattoo, no reward. The referral has to convert to a completed, paid session. Consultations and deposits alone don't trigger the payout.
How do I get my money?
Your reward shows up in your WOTS wallet on the dashboard. When you're ready to cash out, just let us know and we'll send it via Venmo or Zelle. Or convert it to Blood Money and spend it at the studio for 1.5× the value.
